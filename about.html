About FrameTheory

A studio for deliberate software.

We're a small team of editors, colorists, and engineers building the tools we wished existed when we sat down to cut. No bloat, no buzzwords — just plugins that earn their place in your workflow.

Our Mission

To build the most thoughtful, performant, and well-supported plugins available for modern video post — and to grow a coherent ecosystem of them, not a sprawl of one-offs.

How We Work

Every plugin shares a common engine, a common visual language, and a common philosophy: do one thing, do it exceptionally well, and respect the artist's time.

Our Values

What we refuse to compromise on.

Quality

If it doesn't beat the alternative, we don't ship it.

Performance

Profiled, tuned, and tested on real timelines — not demo reels.

Reliability

Predictable, deterministic, and stable across renders.

Support

Real people, real answers, on real timelines.

Our Story

From a single tool to a growing ecosystem.

FrameTheory started as one plugin built to solve our own grading problem. The shared engine that powered it became the foundation for everything we ship now, and every plugin in our lineup is built on that same core.
